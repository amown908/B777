Left Overhead Circuit Breaker Panel

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Contains circuit breakers protecting systems for Emer Lights, Comm WFL, Flt Control, Nav, Capt Displays, and primary left-side cockpit avionics lines.

Right Overhead Circuit Breaker Panel

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Contains circuit breakers protecting right-side electrical paths including systems for Interphone, Air Conditioning loops, Fuel pumps, Engine modules, and Co-Pilot subsystems.

ADIRU System Control

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Manages the Air Data Inertial Reference Unit switch matrix and houses the guarded disconnect toggle for the Fly-By-Wire system Primary Flight Computers.

Electrical Power Control Panel

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Primary electrical node. Controls Battery, APU Gen, Engine Generators, Bus Ties, and External Power connections.

Emergency Lights & Passenger Oxygen

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Controls emergency path lighting deployment toggle, guarded passenger cabin Oxygen Mask Deployment, and contains anti-fogging/anti-icing element controllers for the flight deck windshields.

Hydraulic & Ram Air Control Panel

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Controls Left/Right Engine driven pumps, Central electric system switches, and deployment mechanisms for the Ram Air Turbine (RAT).

Engine Start & Cargo Fire Panel

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Houses left/right pneumatic starter knobs, autostart configurations, and APU / Cargo Fire Discharge systems.

Fuel Pumps & Fuel Jettison Panel

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Manages wing tank/center tank crossfeed selectors alongside the active fuel dump/jettison computer configurations.

Environmental & Air Conditioning Pack Panel

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Manages cabin zone target temperatures, recirculation fan assemblies, and system cooling pack automation switches.

Bleed Air & Pressurization Control

P/N[Enter P/N Later]
Controls Engine isolation bleed valves, APU air source supply, and dual channel manual/auto landing altitude air pressurization profiles.

Captain Controls Node

Houses Left Wiper configurations and instrument console/camera brightness dimmers.

Passenger Signs & Internal Lighting

Manages Seatbelts/No Smoking illumination commands and cockpit interior dome/storm lights.

Anti-Ice & Exterior Lighting Selector

Controls Wing/Engine induction anti-ice and external lighting toggles (Beacon, Nav, Logo, Strobe, Landing/Taxi arrays).

First Officer Control Node

Houses Right Windshield Wiper control assembly parameters.
